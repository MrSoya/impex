x-style支持不同的写法：
  • <div x-style="{'font-size': valExp}">...</div>
  • <div x-style="{'fontSize': valExp}">...</div>
  • <div x-style="font-size:10px;color:red;">...</div>
  • <div x-style="obj">...</div>

demo 1

<div x-style=" {'color':color,'font-size':size}"> ... </div>
color:{{color}} size:{{size}}
color: size:

demo 2

<div x-style=" style"> ... </div>
{{style => json}}
style.color: style.fontSize:

demo 3

<div x-style=" css"> ... </div>
{{css => json}}
